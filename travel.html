Travel & Accomodations

The wedding will be held at the Chinese Scholar's Garden in Snug Harbor Cultural Center, on Staten Island.

We have hotel blocks at the Parker in Manhattan and the Hilton Garden Inn in Staten Island. We will have shuttle service to and from the venue from both hotels.

If you'd like to make your own way, the venue is accessible via public transit - it's about a 30 minute walk or a 10 minute bus ride from the Staten Island Ferry terminal.

Parker

This is a gorgeous space in the middle of the hip and happening Chelsea neighborhood in Manhattan. Correspondingly the prices are higher and the transit time to the wedding will be slightly longer (about a 50 minute drive).

Hilton Garden Inn

This hotel is located closer to the venue, on Staten Island itself. The area is much quieter and it's about a 20 minute drive from the venue.
