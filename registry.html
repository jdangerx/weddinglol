Registry

We understand that it's important to you to express your well wishes, and we welcome them! Some of you may want to give us gifts. The sentiment is appreciated; however, we have more than enough for our own lives, and it would give us great joy if you donated to one of the following organizations instead:

The Bronx Freedom Fund (Donate)

These folks do great work helping people pay bail so they can get back to their jobs and families while awaiting trial. Let us know about your donation so we can properly thank you!

East Harlem Health Outreach Partnership (Donate)

Emily has spent a ton of time volunteering here and helping provide healthcare to uninsured patients in East Harlem. Let us know about your donation so we can properly thank you!

East Harlem Tutorial Program (Donate)

EHTP provides several educational programs for youth in East Harlem with an eye towards preparing them for college. John has spent some time volunteering with their high school robotics team. Let us know about your donation so we can properly thank you!
